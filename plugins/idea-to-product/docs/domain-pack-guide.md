# Domain Pack Guide

Domain packs live outside the core repo.

Use a wrapper repo when a company or team wants domain-specific question banks, policy candidates, measurement candidates, wireframe patterns, and MCP suggestions.

## Required Files

- `domain.yaml`
- `glossary.md`
- `question-bank.md`
- `policy-checklist.md`
- `measurement-checklist.md`
- `wireframe-patterns.md`
- `design-handoff-notes.md`
- `mcp-suggestions.md`
- `reference-sources.md`

## Rule

Domain packs provide question candidates. They do not confirm requirements.

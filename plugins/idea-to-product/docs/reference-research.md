# Reference Research

Reference research and competitive research are support layers.

They are not the product and not a market research report.

## Use Research To Create

- better questions
- policy candidates
- state value candidates
- permission policy candidates
- measurement/logging candidates
- MVP judgment
- v2 or excluded-scope judgment
- AC candidates

## Rules

- Do not copy competitors.
- Keep Core Intent visible.
- Distinguish source facts from inference.
- Record source, check date, observed pattern, application decision, and limitation.
- If access is unavailable, mark the area as 미확인.

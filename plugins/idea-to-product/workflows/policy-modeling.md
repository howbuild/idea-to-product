# Policy Modeling Workflow

## Purpose

Extract policies that developers and operators need before implementation.

## Policy Types

- permission policy
- state policy
- input policy
- restriction policy
- notification policy
- search/filter policy
- operation policy
- exception handling policy
- data retention policy

## Steps

1. Identify actions in the wireframe.
2. Identify who can see, create, edit, delete, approve, reject, or change state.
3. Identify states and transitions.
4. Identify exceptions.
5. Identify button exposure conditions.
6. Connect policies to `data-req-id`, AC, and measurement IDs.
7. Mark unresolved policies.

## Output

Use `templates/policy.md`.

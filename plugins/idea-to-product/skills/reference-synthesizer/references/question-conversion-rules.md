# Question Conversion Rules

Convert research into:

- question candidates
- policy candidates
- state candidates
- permission candidates
- measurement/logging candidates
- MVP fit judgments
- v2 or excluded-scope candidates
- AC candidates

Question candidates should be easy to understand at first read while preserving useful terms such as PRD, AC, KPI, 권한정책, 상태값, 이벤트, and 로그.

Discard research that does not support product decisions.

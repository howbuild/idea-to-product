# Choice Design Rules

- Choices must show real product, development, or operating tradeoffs.
- Include a simple option, a controlled option, a stricter option, and "잘 모르겠음" when useful.
- Avoid choices that differ only by wording.
- Do not hide implementation consequences.
- Do not push the user toward a choice without explaining why.
- Keep the choice labels concrete enough to understand quickly.
- Do not remove useful terms such as 권한정책, 상태값, 이벤트, 로그, PRD, AC, or KPI just to make the wording simpler.
- When a choice is about flow, make the difference visible: entry/next step, usage amount, or quality rule.

# Policy Taxonomy

- Permission policy: who can see or act.
- State policy: allowed state values and transitions.
- Input policy: required fields and validation.
- Restriction policy: limits and caps.
- Notification policy: who receives notices and when.
- Search/filter policy: available filters and default sorting.
- Operation policy: admin or operator rules.
- Exception policy: blocked, failed, empty, or permission-denied cases.
- Data retention policy: what is stored and for how long.
